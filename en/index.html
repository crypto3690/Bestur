Useful information before booking

Everything clear to plan your private service

Here is a direct and useful overview of what BESTUR does and how the process works before a reservation is confirmed.

What is BESTUR?

BESTUR is a premium tour operator in San Miguel de Allende that coordinates private transfers, airport transfers, cultural tours, winery routes, wedding transportation, group services and special private routes.

Where does BESTUR operate?

The main operation starts in San Miguel de Allende and connects with Guanajuato, Querétaro, Mexico City, key airports and different points across the Bajío region.

What services does BESTUR offer?

Private transfers, airport arrivals and departures, private tours, winery tours, wedding transportation, group transportation, family transportation and special services based on the travel plan.

Who is it ideal for?

International travelers, couples, families, groups, wedding guests, premium travelers and clients looking for a clear, comfortable and reliable private service.

How do I request a quote?

The fastest way is to complete the form with your date, service, passengers and specific details. Then you can send it by WhatsApp for a direct response.

How is a reservation confirmed?

A reservation is confirmed once BESTUR validates availability, route, schedule, suggested vehicle and final service conditions.
